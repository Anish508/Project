Complete Project Overview
Project Title

Vendor Invoice Intelligence System: Freight Cost Prediction & Invoice Risk Flagging using Machine Learning

Problem Statement

Companies receive thousands of invoices from different vendors every month. Due to manual verification:

Freight costs are difficult to estimate accurately.
Duplicate or fraudulent invoices may go unnoticed.
Unexpected logistics expenses reduce profit.
High-risk vendors are identified too late.
Financial teams spend significant time auditing invoices.

This project develops an intelligent system that predicts freight costs and flags risky invoices using machine learning.

Business Objectives
1. Predict Freight Cost

Estimate the freight amount before shipment using historical invoice data.

2. Detect Invoice Risk

Identify invoices that are likely to contain anomalies, fraud, or pricing issues.

3. Business Dashboard

Provide finance teams with real-time analytics, risk alerts, and spending insights.

Expected Outcomes
Freight Cost Prediction: Forecast transportation costs to improve budgeting and procurement planning.
Invoice Risk Detection: Automatically identify potentially fraudulent, duplicate, or anomalous invoices.
Vendor Performance Insights: Analyze supplier reliability, spending patterns, and freight efficiency.
Business Intelligence Dashboard: Deliver interactive visualizations and downloadable reports for finance and procurement teams.
Production-Ready Deployment: Expose ML models through FastAPI and provide an easy-to-use Streamlit interface.

This project demonstrates the complete machine learning lifecycle—from SQL-based feature engineering and exploratory analysis to model development, API creation, dashboard visualization, and deployment—making it a strong final-year or portfolio project for data science and machine learning roles.
